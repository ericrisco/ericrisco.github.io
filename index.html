MindWellness

MindWellness consists in to automate a multi-sensorial cabin with the help of one iPad, one Arduino and one server. All data (text, videos, images…) are in the server, and the iPad’s application has to connect with the server and it has to take a specific information. This information is processed with the iPad and it has to sent commands to the Arduino. Finally the Arduino executes these actions in the hardware of the cabin.
